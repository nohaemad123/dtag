THE WHOLE PROCESS OF HOW WE WORK

The development cycle in DTAG starts with a discussion with the client about needs and expectations of a project and continues through installation and operational support.

1- THE DEVELOPMENT CYCLE IN DTAG

starts with a discussion with the client about needs and expectations of a project and continues through installation and operational support. Just as important, the project is not closed until our client has started using the developed software and satissfied enough with the results they desired. We’re in constant communication to build exactly what you want. Our software practices mean, you see progress every day and can prioritize features, You set the milestones and our software does the math.

2- PROJECT MANAGEMENT

Software development process, is focused on meeting the specific requirements and wishes of the customer, choosing optimal solutions for quality services, as well as minimization of material and time resources required for the project.

3- PLANNING & ANALYSIS

For the efficient allocation of time and material resources, as well as for providing the most transparent conditions for cooperation, DTAG performs software development planning, including the following steps: * determine the general concept of the project and purpose of the software; * calculate the required material and time resources; * choosing the best methodology of application development; * determinethe frequency and form of project reports; * determine possible risks associated with the software development and ways to control them.

4- DESIGN

Based on your input, our design team generates wireframes, visualizations and prototypes that give you the best sense of the end-user experience. by the conclusion of this stage, our clients have a deep understanding of what their software will look like and how it will perform.

5- CODING

The project is divided into discreet segments as the software code is tested and revised accordingly. With each new iteration, features can be added based on your feedback and tested until they’re fully functional. Throughout this process, we make sure to continually update our clients on the project status and inform them of all the milestones we’ve passed.

6- TESTING & QUALITY CONTROL

After optimization is complete and all the features finalized, we begin stringently testing the software one last time. Any bugs or performance-issues are eliminated until the product is running smoothly. Based on feedback from our Quality Control managers.

7- DEPLOYMENT

Releasing your software. By this point, DTAG has done its research and thoroughly understands your target consumer, and we work hard to ensure that your product gets to its intended users and achieves maximum market penetration.

8- MAINTENANCE

Just because we’ve designed and deployed your product doesn’t mean our job is over. Dtag is there to help with updates, queries and general consultation. Our professional support staff will assist in any way they can
